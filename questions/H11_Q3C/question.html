Status Register

What is the value of the Z bit after executing the following sequence of instructions?

MOV R{{params.r0}}, #0 MOVS R{{params.r1}}, R{{params.r0}}

The Zero (Z) bit will be 1, since moving R{{params.r0}} into R{{params.r1}} with MOVS will give a value of 0 in the result register (R2). Thus the Zero bit/flag will be set to 1.
